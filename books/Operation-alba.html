UCU: Operation Alba

Psychological action in a world where your follower count decides your rights - and zero followers means you’re prey.

Genre: Psychological Action Format: Serial (ongoing) System: Followers → Privilege

Premise

Society runs on visibility: followers aren’t money - they’re legal recognition. Drop too low and doors close. Hit zero and you become “Unregistered,” hunted by government Retrievers.

World Snapshot

  • Tiers & Lanes: Skyline (VIP) → Mid → Sub → Shadow → Zero.
  • Surveillance: retro-futuristic clockwork monitoring makes disappearance nearly impossible.
  • Retrievers: elite agents tasked with capturing zero-follower “Zeros.”
